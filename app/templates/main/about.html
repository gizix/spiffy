About Spiffy

Spiffy is a web application that helps you visualize and explore your Spotify listening habits in new ways. Need a new shuffle? Easily create custom tailored playlists with our Randomizer feature.

Features

  • Connect to your Spotify account securely
  • Analyze your top tracks and artists
  • Explore your playlists and saved songs
  • Visualize your music preferences with interactive charts
  • Keep your data private with local storage
  • Randomizer: Create customized shuffled playlists with advanced filtering
    • Filter tracks by artist limit, duration, release year, and more
    • Save shuffle configurations for future use
    • Track and manage playlist creation history
    • Create playlists from either your saved tracks or existing playlists

How It Works

Spiffy uses the official Spotify Web API to fetch data about your listening habits. Your data is stored in a local SQLite database, so you maintain complete control over your information.

The Randomizer creates smarter shuffled playlists by applying customizable rules to your music library. Unlike Spotify's basic shuffle, our system lets you control exactly how your playlists are generated, with options to limit artists, balance music from different eras, and create the perfect mix for any occasion. Other than the random number generator, no other algorithms are used to shuffle your tracks for a truly random shuffle.

Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Your Spotify data is stored locally and is not shared with any third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Technologies Used

  • Python with Flask web framework
  • SQLite for data storage
  • Spotipy for Spotify API integration
  • Chart.js for data visualization
  • Bootstrap for responsive design
